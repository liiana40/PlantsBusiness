Magic Plants

How plants make us feel

Plants have a long-term positive effect on moods. People start feeling less depressed and anxious after receiving plants, and radiate a higher sense of enjoyment and life satisfaction. Plans make awesome connections. When you plant the seeds, plants do not grow fully and blooms overnight. You have to nurture them day by day and wait for the beauty and calming effects on you by letting it sprout buds and bloom naturally.

The way a flower embraces a new day can be a real inspiration for us. Before the first rays of sunshine hits the treetop, the flower rises from its slumber, opening its petals. The dew drops fall on the plant. Once it opens its petals, the flower never closes them even an inch till it gets dark and goes to sleep.
